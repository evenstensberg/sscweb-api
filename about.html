April - July 2016

  • A powerful new API for building plugins to extend Open MCT’s capabilities
  • New plotting types and user options for controlling the appearance of plotted telemetry
  • Consolidation of the concepts of ‘historical’ and ‘real-time’ telemetry to avoid the need to context-switch between the two.

August - December 2016

  • Improved mobile and gesture support.
  • New options for building powerful user-composed layouts with additional options to apply labels, lines, and other drawing features.
  • New ways of editing views and objects to make the process more seamless, and to allow editing of objects directly within layouts.
  • A new style guide, that in addition to the controls and platform features provided by Open MCT will allow developers to extend Open MCT while remaining consistent to its look-and-feel

2017 And Onwards

  • Improved support for all major browsers
  • New features such as an operator notebook and annotations for data analysis, new visualizations, and platform tools.
